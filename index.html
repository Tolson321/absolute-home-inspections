WingKee BBQ

Since 1986 Wingkee BBQ has provided Rhode Island the authentic Far East Roast Duck. The Wingkee BBQ roast duck has been well known in the Tri-state area for over 30 years. We only use the highest quality duck with our secret traditional recipe.
